QR Codes On The Fly

ECC Level: 1 2 3 4

Usage

  • Include the AngularJS library and wpk.qrcode.js.
  • Add CSS for .qrcode and .qrcode.clickable.
  • Add 'wpk.qrcode' to your app's dependencies.
  • Embed with
    <qrcode data="text" ecclevel="ecc"></qrcode>

The scope variables text and ecc are now bound and watched and the QR code automatically updates whenever either of them changes.
